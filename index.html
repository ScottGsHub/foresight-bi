📐 How Composite Probability is Calculated

The Composite probability is a weighted average of available market prices, prioritizing real-money markets over play-money platforms.

Weighting Formula

🥇

3× weight

🥈

🥉

1× weight

Why this weighting? Real-money markets have stronger incentives for accuracy. Kalshi is US-regulated with higher stakes. Polymarket has deep liquidity but uses crypto. Manifold is great for niche questions but uses play money, so gets lowest weight.
